Dental Sales Agentic Flows

Three scenario types to be stored under .ai/samples/

Scenario 1: 🍽️ Friendly Sales Visit (Lunch Expense)

🍽️ Agent takes dentist to lunch — $187
↓
📝 Submit expense report
↓
🔀 Per Diem Check: $100 limit
$187 > $100 → Over limit
↓
✅ Auto-Approved
(would route here if ≤ $100)
↓
👤 Human-in-the-Loop Approval
Manager reviews & approves/rejects

Scenario 2: 🔧 Scheduled Maintenance Visit

📅 Scheduled trigger: Maintenance due
↓
🤖 AI schedules visit with customer
↓
🔀 Match visit to maintenance schedule
↓
🔧 Human agent services equipment
↓
✅ Complete & log service record

Scenario 3: ⚠️ Recall Flow

⚠️ Recall initiated for X100 equipment
↓
🤖 AI orders replacement parts
↓
📅 AI schedules maintenance visit
↓
⛔ Precondition: Parts must have arrived
Flow blocks here until parts are delivered
↓
🔧 Human agent executes recall (parts on hand)
↓
✅ Recall completed & documented

These flows will be defined as structured JSON in .ai/samples/ and routed by a lightweight AI model (Qwen 3.5 27B) based on incoming "email" triggers.
